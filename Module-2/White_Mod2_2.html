Chapter 1: A little about myself as a developer.

My name is Zach, and I'm originally from Arizona, where I grew up surrounded by desert heat, cacti, and endless sunshine. The dry, warm climate shaped a lot of who I am — there's something about growing up in the desert that teaches you resilience and adaptability. These days, I've traded the Southwest scenery for the four seasons of Ohio, and while the winters took some getting used to, I've genuinely grown to love the change of pace and the beauty each season brings.

Professionally, I work as an Optician, a career I find deeply rewarding because at the end of the day, I'm helping people see the world more clearly. One of my proudest accomplishments has been contributing to the development of my doctor's EHR (Electronic Health Records) system, which allowed me to merge my technical curiosity with my passion for improving healthcare. That experience gave me a unique perspective on both the clinical and tech sides of the industry, and it's a big part of what inspired me to pursue software development more seriously.

When it comes to hobbies, gaming is a huge part of my life — I fell in love with the world of software and technology through my time as a functional tester and reporter at Microsoft Gaming Studios. I spend a lot of time in Final Fantasy XIV and have a deep love for ARPGs like Path of Exile and Diablo, where the loot-hunting grind never gets old. That hands-on experience in the gaming industry gave me a real appreciation for what goes into building great software, which is ultimately what drew me to coding.

On the more creative and hands-on side, I build and paint Gundam model kits and miniature figures using an airbrush, a hobby that demands patience, precision, and a steady hand. There's something incredibly satisfying about taking a raw kit and transforming it into a finished, painted piece — it's a great counterbalance to the fast-paced world of gaming. Between my love of gaming, model building, and now learning Java, I'm someone who thrives when I'm creating, building, or solving something, and I can't wait to bring that same energy into this class!
